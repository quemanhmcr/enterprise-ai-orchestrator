# Sample Company Policy Document

## Vacation Policy

### Annual Leave Entitlement

All full-time employees are entitled to:
- 15 days of paid vacation per year in the first 3 years
- 20 days of paid vacation per year after 3 years of service
- 25 days of paid vacation per year after 5 years of service

### Requesting Time Off

1. Submit vacation requests at least 2 weeks in advance
2. Requests must be approved by direct manager
3. Use the company HR portal for all requests
4. Maximum consecutive vacation days: 10 days (without special approval)

### Carryover Policy

- Up to 5 unused vacation days can be carried over to the next year
- Carryover days must be used within the first quarter
- No compensation for unused vacation days

## Work From Home Policy

### Eligibility

- Available to all employees after 90-day probation period
- Role must be suitable for remote work
- Manager approval required

### Guidelines

- Maximum 2 days per week for WFH
- Must maintain regular working hours
- Must be available for video calls
- Home workspace must meet ergonomic standards

### Equipment

Company provides:
- Laptop and necessary peripherals
- VPN access for secure connection
- Monthly internet allowance: $50

## Employee Benefits

### Health Insurance

- Medical insurance coverage: 100% for employee, 50% for dependents
- Dental insurance: Available as optional add-on
- Vision insurance: Included in medical coverage

### Retirement Plan

- 401(k) matching: Company matches up to 5% of salary
- Vesting schedule: 100% vested after 3 years
- Financial planning services available

### Professional Development

- Annual training budget: $2,000 per employee
- Conference attendance: Up to 2 conferences per year
- Internal training programs available quarterly
